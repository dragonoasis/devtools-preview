Camera Recorder — Webcam Video Capture

Free online camera recorder. Record video from your webcam with optional audio, pause/resume, and download as WebM. No sign-up, runs in your browser.

Click "Start Camera" to begin

What Is the Camera Recorder Tool?

This camera recorder lets you capture video directly from your webcam in the browser using the MediaRecorder API. You can record with or without audio, pause and resume recording, and download the result as a WebM file. All processing happens locally — no video data is uploaded to any server.

How to Record Video

  1. Click Start Camera and grant camera access when prompted.
  2. Toggle Include Audio on or off before recording.
  3. Click Record to start capturing video. A timer shows elapsed time.
  4. Use Pause/Resume to temporarily stop recording.
  5. Click Stop to finish recording and preview the result.
  6. Click Download Recording to save the video as a .webm file.

Common Use Cases

  • Quick Screen Recordings — Record a quick video message or demo without installing any software.
  • Bug Report Videos — Capture yourself demonstrating a bug or issue for a development team.
  • Content Creation — Record quick webcam clips for social media or presentations.

FAQ

Is my video uploaded to any server?
No. All recording happens entirely in your browser using the MediaRecorder API. Video data never leaves your device.
What video format is used?
Videos are recorded in WebM format (VP9 codec when available). Most modern browsers and video players support WebM.
Can I choose which camera to use?
The tool uses the default front-facing camera. If you have multiple cameras, your browser may offer a selection in the permission prompt.
