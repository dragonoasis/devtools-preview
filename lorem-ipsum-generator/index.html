What Is a Lorem Ipsum Generator?

A Lorem Ipsum generator creates placeholder text based on a scrambled excerpt from Cicero's 'De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum' (45 BC). This dummy text has been used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s as a standard filler, allowing designers and developers to focus on layout and visual design without being distracted by meaningful content.

How to Use the Lorem Ipsum Generator

  1. Set the number of units you want to generate using the count field.
  2. Choose the unit type: paragraphs, sentences, or individual words.
  3. The placeholder text generates automatically as you adjust the settings.
  4. Review the word count displayed below the controls.
  5. Click the copy button to copy the generated text to your clipboard.

FAQ

Why use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text?
Lorem Ipsum provides a natural distribution of letters and word lengths similar to English, making layouts look realistic. Using real text can distract reviewers from evaluating the design itself, as they may focus on reading the content instead.
Is Lorem Ipsum actual Latin?
Lorem Ipsum is derived from sections of 'De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum' by Cicero, written in 45 BC. However, the standard version has been altered and scrambled over the centuries, so it is not proper Latin and has no coherent meaning.
What is the maximum amount of text I can generate?
You can generate up to 100 units (paragraphs, sentences, or words) at a time. For larger amounts, simply generate multiple batches and combine them as needed.
